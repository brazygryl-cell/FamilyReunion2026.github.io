Where We'll Stay

Villatel Orlando Resort offers private family villas (4–9 bedrooms), each with a pool, game room, and kitchen — designed for togetherness and comfort.

  • Private spa pools, game rooms, and full kitchens.
  • Spacious family layouts with modern amenities.
  • Villa assignments shared after final payments.
Visit Villatel Orlando Intl (MCO)

Getting There

  • Orlando Intl (MCO): 25–35 min drive to resort.
  • Sanford (SFB): Smaller airport (~1 hr drive).
  • Shuttles: Mears Connect & van services available.
  • Car Rentals: Best for flexible family travel.

Before You Travel

  • Bring a Real ID or passport for TSA check-in.
  • Share your flight info for shuttle coordination.
  • Book private vans early for large family groups.
