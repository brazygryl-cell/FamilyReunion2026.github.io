Getting There

Most guests will fly into Orlando International Airport (MCO). It’s a short 25–35 minute drive to the resort.

Airports

  • MCO (Orlando Intl): Closest airport, best option for most guests.
  • SFB (Sanford): Smaller airport with some low-cost carriers.

Transfers

  • Shared Shuttle: Mears Connect offers shared and private shuttles from MCO.
  • Private Van: Ideal for larger families — we’ll help coordinate options.

Rental Cars

Renting is recommended for families planning local trips. Major brands operate at MCO — book vans early!

Travel Checklist

  • ✅ Bring a Real ID or valid passport for TSA.
  • 🕒 Share your arrival times to help us coordinate shuttles.
  • 🚐 Request private vans early if traveling in a large group.
